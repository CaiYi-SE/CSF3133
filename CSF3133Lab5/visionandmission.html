UMT’S PHILOSOPHY

The integration of knowledge and practice based on the faith in Allah is fundamental to the university’s endeavour in preparing competent human capital for global prosperity.

VISION

A marine-focused university, reputed nationally and respected globally.

MISSION

Generating knowledge for the prosperity of the community and world sustainability.

EDUCATIONAL GOALS

Producing competent graduates who possess the value of ihsan for society’s well-being and environmental sustainability.

SLOGAN

Ocean of Discoveries, for Global Sustainability.

NICHE AREA

Marine Science and Aquatic Resources.

FUNCTIONS

  • Supports the mission of the university to become the premier centre of learning and research by contributing to human progress and knowledge exploration.
  • Provides trained manpower with professional skills, high discipline, and positive ethics.
  • Produces graduates who are sensitive to management ideas, responsive to change, and become role models for society.
  • Serves society by sharing new ideas, practices, and solutions to current issues.
  • Strengthens relationships with other universities, institutions, and industries for mutual benefit and national development.
  • Supports the mission of the university as an organised centre of research, learning, service, and technological exploration.

OBJECTIVES

  • Explores all areas related to Science, Technology, and Natural Resource Management through research.
  • Provides advanced facilities to support knowledge development, learning, and scholarship.
  • Offers excellent programmes that meet current and future needs.
  • Produces graduates who are responsible, knowledgeable, confident, and competent.
  • Contributes to social, economic, and educational development through community-focused services.
